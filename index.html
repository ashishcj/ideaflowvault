Ad Networks & Payout Integrations

Configure your display ads and set up your preferred payment method: PayPal, Payoneer, Wise, or Direct Bank Transfer.

Google AdSense serves text and banner ads automatically. AdSense pays via direct wire transfer, bank deposit (direct deposit), or physical checks once you hit their minimum payment threshold ($100).

1. Header Script Integration

HTML Header Code 
<!-- Paste this block inside the <head> tags of your site -->
<script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js?client=ca-pub-YOUR_PUBLISHER_ID"
     crossorigin="anonymous"></script>

2. Body Ad Slot Container

HTML Body Placement 
<!-- Place this ad block container where the banner should show -->
<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:block"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-YOUR_PUBLISHER_ID"
     data-ad-slot="YOUR_AD_SLOT_ID"
     data-ad-format="auto"
     data-full-width-responsive="true"></ins>
<script>
     (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
</script>

Ezoic is a display ad network that fully supports direct payouts to PayPal, Payoneer, and Wise (via international bank transfers).

Alternative Payment Options on Ezoic:

  • Payoneer: Link your Ezoic dashboard directly to Payoneer. It offers extremely low conversion fees and allows you to withdraw funds directly in your local currency.
  • Wise (Direct Bank Transfer): Enter the bank details provided by Wise (Multi-currency Account routing and account numbers) into the Bank Transfer field in Ezoic to receive low-fee transfers.
  • Direct Deposit (ACH/Wire): Standard payouts directly to your local bank account.
Ezoic Integration Tag 
<!-- Place Ezoic header placeholder script tag -->
<script type="text/javascript">
var ezoicId = YOUR_EZOIC_ID; 
</script>
<script type="text/javascript" src="//g.ezoic.net/ezoic/ezoic.js" async></script>

In addition to programmatic display ads, you can embed direct support buttons from platforms like Stripe Payment Links, Buy Me A Coffee, or Ko-fi to receive instant payouts.

Embeddable Stripe/Custom Support Link Button

HTML Button Link 
<!-- Simple HTML Support Button linking to your Stripe or BuyMeACoffee Page -->
<a href="https://buy.stripe.com/YOUR_STRIPE_LINK_OR_BUYMEACOFFEE_URL" target="_blank" 
   style="display: inline-block; background-color: #2563eb; color: #ffffff; padding: 0.75rem 1.5rem; text-decoration: none; border-radius: 6px; font-weight: 600; font-family: sans-serif;">
    Support My Work
</a>
