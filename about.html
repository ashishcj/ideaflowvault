About Ideaflow Vault

Welcome to Ideaflow Vault (accessible from https://ideaflowvault.com), the free, open, and private workspace built specifically for side hustle website builders and digital creators.

Our Mission

Launching a niche website or display ad project is a balancing act. Too often, creators spend hundreds of dollars on premium SEO tools or web hosting before validating whether their niche has profitable cost-per-click (CPC) rates or sufficient traffic volume to survive.

Ideaflow was built to bridge this gap by offering:

  • High-Yield Niche Blueprints: Free, researched blueprints comparing average traffic potential and CPC rates across categories.
  • Dynamic Revenue Projection: An interactive calculator that projects daily, monthly, and yearly display ad payouts based on views, Click-Through Rates (CTR), and Cost-Per-Click (CPC).
  • 100% Free Resources: Step-by-step guides for connecting major ad networks like Google AdSense, implementing affiliate promotions, choosing reliable web hosts, and setting up international bank transfers.

100% Private Local Vault

We believe your ideas are yours alone. Ideaflow is built using serverless utility models. Any keywords or niche ideas you input into the "My Research Vault" dashboard are logged strictly in your web browser's local storage (LocalStorage). No accounts are required, and no data is harvested or uploaded to external servers.

Who Built Ideaflow?

Ideaflow was founded and is maintained by Ashish Divakar. As a side hustle builder and content publisher, Ashish noticed a lack of simple, quick-to-load planning dashboards that help builders visualize display ad and affiliate returns before purchasing a domain. Ideaflow was created as a passion project to give publishers the tools they need to succeed.
