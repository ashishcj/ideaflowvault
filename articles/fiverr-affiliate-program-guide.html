How the Fiverr Affiliate Program Works

Fiverr is the world’s largest marketplace for freelance services, from graphic design and copy writing to complex software engineering. As a Fiverr Affiliate, you earn a commission by referring **First-Time Buyers (FTBs)** to the platform.

When a visitor clicks your unique affiliate tracking link and makes a purchase, Fiverr attributes that transaction to you and pays a percentage or flat fee depending on the service category. Payouts are made monthly via PayPal, Payoneer, or Wire Transfer once you hit the $100 minimum threshold.

Fiverr CPA vs. Hybrid: Which is Better?

When you sign up, you get access to two main commission engines. You can choose which tracking link to generate depending on your target audience:

Fiverr CPA

Flat Payout ($15 - $150)

You earn a flat fee based on the service the buyer orders. This is excellent for blogs recommending specific gigs like logo design, SEO audits, or website setup.

Fiverr Hybrid

$10 CPA + 10% Rev Share

You earn a flat $10 fee immediately plus 10% of every order the referred user makes for the next 12 months. Best for long-term clients or SaaS builders.

Step-by-Step Registration Process

Getting started takes under 5 minutes. Approvals are instant for almost all creators:

  1. Access the form: Navigate to the Fiverr Affiliate Partnerships Portal and click **Join Now**.
  2. Fill Account Details: Choose **Individual** account type. Fill out your name, contact information, language, and country.
  3. Define Marketing Methods: Tell Fiverr how you plan to promote their platform. Check boxes for **Content Site**, **Blog**, or **Social Media**.
  4. Agree and Submit: Check the terms box and click register. Your account is approved immediately, and you can log in to your dashboard.

How to Generate Deep Links (CPA)

Recommending the general Fiverr homepage has low conversion. The secret is **Deep Linking** — linking directly to a specific search query or a pre-selected high-rated freelancer. Here is how to create deep links:

Step-by-Step Deep Link Generation

  1. Navigate to Fiverr.com and search for a highly relevant service (e.g. *\"WordPress development\"* or *\"SEO keyword research\"*). Copy the URL of that category page.
  2. Log in to your **Fiverr Affiliate Dashboard**.
  3. In the sidebar navigation, click on **Marketing Tools** ➔ **Default and Deep Links**.
  4. Look for the **LPURL** button (Landing Page URL) on the upper right side of the list. Click it, paste the URL you copied, and click Enter.
  5. Your tracking link list will update. Copy the link in the **Fiverr CPA** row. This link will redirect users to that specific category while tracking your commission!

Strategies to Reach $1,000/Month

To make $1,000/month, focus on specific high-converting zones on your niche website rather than generic banners:

  • Contextual Tool Lists: In your tools list on the homepage or ad-calculator page, place a direct link to Fiverr freelancing under *"Recommended Tools"*. Many users calculating ad revenues are launching websites and need writers, logo designers, or developers.
  • Niche Directory Integration: Integrate Fiverr links directly on your Niche Directory profiles. For example, beside the *"AI Trip Planner"* preset, list affiliate CTAs linking to *"Hire travel planners on Fiverr"* or *"Hire travel blog writers"*. This targets users at the exact moment they are reviewing niche difficulty.
  • Write Case Studies: Share real examples of how a freelancer on Fiverr helped build or design part of your digital assets. Real reviews convert at a 3x higher rate than generic affiliate link placements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I refer existing Fiverr users?

No, the Fiverr Affiliate program only attributes commissions for First-Time Buyers (FTBs). However, since Fiverr is constantly adding new categories, many new users register daily.

What is the minimum payout threshold?

The minimum payout threshold is $100. Payouts are generated once a month once your approved commissions exceed this limit.
